## HOBIC Token (HBC) Whitepaper

### Introduction
HOBIC is a BEP-20 utility token on Binance Smart Chain designed to support community-based hobby events. As the native token of the HOBICON ecosystem, HOBIC facilitates fundraising, staking, and access to event-related rewards.

### Vision
To connect hobby communities through digital assets that reward participation and build long-term engagement via tokenized experiences.

### Use Cases
- **Fundraising for Events**: Supports offline events and community activities.
- **Merchandise Access**: Exclusive items redeemable via HOBIC.
- **Staking & Rewards**: Earn rewards through holding and staking HOBIC.
- **Community Engagement**: Incentivize participation through digital campaigns.

### Token Details
- **Name**: HOBIC Token
- **Symbol**: HBC
- **Chain**: Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 HBC
- **Decimals**: 18

### Smart Contract
- **No Mint Function**
- **No Admin / Owner**
- **No Blacklist**
- **Burnable by Holders**
- **EIP-2612 Permit Supported**

### Ecosystem
HOBIC will initially support HOBICON, a multiverse hobby convention, and expand to various hobby-based projects. Future integrations include merchandise platforms, staking dashboards, and gamified community quests.
